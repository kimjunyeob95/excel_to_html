What is Visual
Merchandising?

It is a retail strategy that constructs brand image
and delivers pleasant customer experiences
through various in-store elements.

The main objective of visual merchandising is to
attract customers into the store, encourage them
to engage with the products, and motivate
the customer towards making a purchase.

Why is Visual
Merchandising
important?

  • Provides product information and
    hands-on opportunities for
    product trial.

  • Encourages the shoppers to
    explore and provides a positive
    shopping experience.

  • Acts as a brand communication
    channel and redefines the
    product story.

How does Samsung do
Visual Merchandising?

Samsung visual merchandising strategy
comes from the store design concept based
on brand identity.

1. Brand Identity

Samsung brand identity consists of 4 unique
personalities: Bold, Innovative, Inviting,
Engineered.

BOLD INNOVATIVE INVITING ENGINEERED

These brand values are applied to Samsung
retail store’s visual elements to promote the
products.

2. Store Design Concept

Samsung’s store design concept, The Pioneer’s
Playground, uses elements and displays to
attract the customers and encourage them to
explore the products freely.

Samsung Visual
Merchandising Elements
